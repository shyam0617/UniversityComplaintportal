About RGUKT Complaint Portal

Welcome to the RGUKT Complaint Portal, where your voice matters!

Our Mission

We strive to provide a reliable platform for submitting and resolving grievances within RGUKT. Our mission is to enhance transparency and efficiency in addressing complaints, ensuring a positive and supportive environment for all members of our community.

What We Offer

The RGUKT Complaint Portal offers an intuitive interface for submitting complaints, real-time tracking of complaint status, and seamless communication with university authorities. We are committed to delivering timely updates and maintaining fairness throughout the complaint resolution process.

Key Features

  • User-friendly interface for submitting complaints
  • Real-time tracking of complaint status
  • Efficient communication with university authorities
  • Transparent and impartial complaint resolution process
  • Timely updates and notifications
